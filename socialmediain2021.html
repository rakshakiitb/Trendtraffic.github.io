 Will social media in 2021s ever rule the marketing world?

-by A Saraswati

In 2021, we will see much influencer-generated content enabling brands and establish brand awareness. Because in 2020 we had a strong start and we will see the impact in 2021. Social Media Marketing will only increase, and it will become a norm to curate UGC( User-generated content). Especially after the attack of COVID-19 on our planet, since everything has been digital and online.
Social media has a huge impact on your business, but social media doesn't impact your business overnight. Building a presence and seeing the ROI will likely take years. Although after the pandemic ways of marketing and communicating with the customers have been changed and modified online. Nowadays, it is a must to have an online presence for your business growth. 

How did corona affect Digital Marketing?

The global pandemic had an impact on every other business. But how did it modify us from traditional marketing style to digital marketing style?
Due to corona, people were afraid to leave their homes, and they started to prefer online shopping.
According to some experts "Digital business pivots may become permanent".The reason is simple. This is because companies have learned lessons in 2020 not only regarding safety concerns but also by going virtual, they opened up their event to an entirely new audience as virtual events are more accessible than in person. For virtual events, no visa issues, travel restrictions, and costs aren't as much of a concern. That means a lower price for more people means higher ROI for your business.

Why should you go online in 2021?

When you look at 2020, it has been very tough for all businesses and companies, especially for offline corporations. I'm sure it's been tough for you too!
We all learned to work from home. We started to connect and communicate with people through the internet by staying in. We began to evolve our businesses from offline to online for our wellbeing..

"Virtual events started of necessity but are here to stay because of ROI and accessibility".
-Forbes

Apps like Facebook and Instagram are making it very easy for customers to shop online. Users can buy anything without ever leaving the app with very few and simple steps

Social media will become a top channel for purchase instead of just discovery.

Social media is not only for browsing and connecting with new people; it's also one of the ways to advertise your products and services by sitting at home. Social media gives you the ability to control your brand's voice to create an engagement with your customers.
Social media plays a significant role in connecting with new customers and developing your brand awareness if done with the right strategy and suitable media.
Yet most businesses and brands will continue to advertise through traditional options like TV advertising or Youtube. Still, most savvy companies will choose social media marketing because it's within budget and accessible.
