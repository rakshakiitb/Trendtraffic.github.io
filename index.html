The team was formed in the year 2015 by Some of the IITB enthusiasts to build drones/planes that can be used for a rescue mission. Being one of the latest introductions to tech teams, Rakshak is still in the developing phase. We compete at different International level competitions like AUVSI-SUAS and we are improving every year. Our task is to build a drone that can be helpful in a rescue operation. The task of the drone is to deliver Commodities to the needy ones, even when it seems impossible. Our drones are autonomous i.e. Operates on their own.

As of now, we have developed five planes. Each year we are trying to stretch our limits by our innovation and teamwork. We would also like to thank IITB for supporting us and providing the platform to showcase our talent at the international level.
