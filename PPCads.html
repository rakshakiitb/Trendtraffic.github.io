How Google, Facebook, Instagram Ads Work For Brands in 2021?

-by Ismail Kolkar

Google Ads or Google Adwords

Before talking about Google ads we have to know what is search engine marketing, Earlier SEM is a broad term used for Search engine optimisation and paid search ads but changed the focus to purely on Paid search ads/Paid search advertising./Pay-per-click/PPC
The types of ads include  

  • Search ads
  • Display ads
  • Search with display select

Google Ads at the Campaign level

Location- It is used for specific geographic areas of the city or country in the world.
Language- it is available in 44 different languages in the world.
Bid strategy- we can use either manual or automated bidding strategies.
Google charges money only when the user clicks on the ad called CPC (cost-per-click) model.
CPM (cost-per-thousand impressions) is a type of payment model when Google charges money for a thousand impressions or ads displayed.
Most search ads are sold on a CPC / PPC basis. Display advertising options are sold on a CPM basis.

Network-Google Ads is an advertising platform that works mainly on search networks(71%traffic) and display networks(reaches 2 billion websites).
Dates- we can choose specific date ranges when we want to run our ad and particular hours of the day.
Google Ads at Ad Group level
Ads-
These may be text ad, image ad, video ad.
Targeting is essential for running successful ads campaigns. Can create ad placement and displayed to the right people at the right time for successful campaigns. Google Ads offers different ways of targeting your ads. 

Audiences-

  1. Demographics
  2. Affinity
  3. In-market
  4. Custom intent
  5. Similar audiences
  6. Remarketing 

Content targeting-.

  1. Topics
  2. Placement
  3. Content keywords

Devices - we can target particular devices like mobile phone, desktop, tablet, known as Ad Interaction device/conversion device.

FACEBOOK ADS

Social media channels like Facebook, Instagram, Linkedin, etc. belong to the partially paid media to leverage these channels for creating free business profiles. Facebook has more than 2.5 billion users on the track.
With over 2.7 billion monthly active users(MAUs) as of the 2nd quarter of 2020, First largest social network worldwide. Over 1.66 billion daily active users (DAUs) as of December 2019, which tends to a 9 % increase year on year.
Facebook has personalised news- feeds specific to the users based on the follower base, liking and commenting on brands' Facebook business pages. We can also get the ads displayed, which means the type of content shown to a user is different from the other if I have liked some movie, video or any kind of content what ads to be displayed based on their connections of 100's of different brands and people. The Facebook algorithm automatically works by featuring the ads from various data signals like interest, activities and type of engagement on the channel. The amplification works based on what user reinforces and what user needs or tunes in the belief system, what kind of communication/comments user does, and any repeated actions performed in a loop in a particular time called Echo-Chamber. Gone those days from 2012 -14 where the reach was drastically reduced, Facebook has decreased the organic reach to less than 1% and wants to monetise its channel by ad placements. Several followers won't work for social media platforms for getting brand exposure and engagement. The engagement nowadays can be done by broadcasting Live stories, Videos which are excellent watch time. Brands have to treat Facebook as an ad platform to add value they can leverage business pages. Don't hard sell on the channel because users are coming to the venue for passing the time and some time to research new products'

Instagram Ads

In 2006, Kevin Sistronki first created the company called Snow, which sells tooth whitening kit. Kevin asked users to upload the photos of whitening of teeth on the website. Later, he developed an app named "Burbon" in January 2010, which features pictures. It has an instant camera and telegram for instant sharing. Due to the above features, it has got the name Instagram.
After 1.5 years he sold his startup to Mark Zukerberg for 100 crore dollars.
Instagram Ads.

Why do users flock to this channel?
Mostly female demographics are higher on this platform for viewing "Picture version of Twitter."
According to Rival IQ social media benchmark report, Engagement on Instagram is more than 1.73%, on Facebook, it is 0.16% and lastly 0.046% on Twitter. Instagram added a feature of stories in spring 2016.
People authenticate this channel to connect with a more in-depth and fast level of image and media than with simple texts that are the same ways influencers see this for product placements.
The videos ranging from 3-60 seconds tends to attract and gain exposure for the audience. If brands want to dig deeper for different posts from different people, wit other hashtags consistently for various products and offerings.
